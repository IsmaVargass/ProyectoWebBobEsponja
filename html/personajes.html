Characters

SpongeBob


A cheerful, optimistic sea sponge who works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and loves jellyfishing and blowing bubbles

  • Optimistic
  • Silly
  • Bad at driving

Patrick Star


SpongeBob's best friend; a lovable, dim-witted starfish known for his simplicity and humorous cluelessness.

  • Great friend
  • Lazy
  • Good at driving

Squidward


A grumpy, cynical octopus and SpongeBob's neighbor. He enjoys playing the clarinet and longs for a life of sophistication away from SpongeBob's antics.

  • Egocentric
  • Complains a lot
  • Horrible at playing the clarinet

Sandy


A smart, athletic squirrel from Texas who lives in an underwater dome. She's scientifically inclined, adventurous, and friendly.

  • Smart
  • Loyal friend
  • Skilled at martial arts

Mr. Krabs


The owner of the Krusty Krab restaurant, obsessively focused on making money. He’s a crab who values money above almost everything else, but has a soft spot for his daughter Pearl.

  • Stingy
  • Ingenious
  • Good heart

Sheldon J. Plankton


The tiny antagonist who continually plots to steal the secret Krabby Patty formula from the Krusty Krab, running the unsuccessful Chum Bucket restaurant.

  • Evil genious
  • Envious
  • Computer as a wife
